About Our Church

AEPC Kabiria is a Bible-believing Reformed congregation committed to faithful preaching of God's Word, discipleship, evangelism, prayer and Christian fellowship.

Our Mission: To glorify God by making disciples of Jesus Christ and serving our community through the Gospel.

One Church, Many Tongues: From English to Kiswahili, Dinka, and Maban — we preach Christ to all people and nations.

Worship

We worship God with Christ-centered songs and heartfelt praise. Our worship team leads us in hymns and contemporary songs in English, Kiswahili, Dinka, and Maban. Every song is chosen to teach Scripture and lift our eyes to Jesus.
